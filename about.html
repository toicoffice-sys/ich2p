About ICH2P 2026

17th International Conference on Hydrogen Production — December 17–19, 2026, Learning Resource Center, De La Salle Lipa, Philippines

Welcome from DLSL

"De La Salle Lipa is honored to host the 17th ICH2P 2026. This conference reflects our commitment to research excellence, international collaboration, and the advancement of sustainable energy technologies."

— Dr. Analiza Resurreccion
Local Organizing Conference Chair, De La Salle Lipa

De La Salle Lipa (DLSL) is a Catholic institution of higher learning in Lipa City, Batangas, Philippines, operating under the charism of St. John Baptist de la Salle. As host of ICH2P 2026, DLSL brings its tradition of academic excellence and international engagement to the global hydrogen research community.

About the Conference

The 17th International Conference on Hydrogen Production (ICH2P 2026) aims to bring together researchers, scientists, academicians, industry specialists, and professionals working in hydrogen energy technologies. It has a key focus on hydrogen ecosystem development and decarbonization, which is a critical priority for many countries.

This unique conference serves as a forum that promulgates ideas, experience, and knowledge among researchers and engineers working on hydrogen production, storage, delivery, distribution, conversion, and utilization technologies across the globe. It will also discuss the challenges, opportunities, future directions, and specific policies and strategies needed to help develop the hydrogen ecosystem.

Conference Focus

ICH2P 2026 covers cutting-edge topics across the hydrogen ecosystem.

Conventional & Renewable Hydrogen Technologies

Production methods, gasification, and solar hydrogen technologies.

Hydrogen Economy & Ecosystem

Infrastructure, delivery, transportation, and policy/regulatory frameworks.

Safety, Storage & Materials

Hydrogen storage, materials, carriers, and risk assessment.

Fuel Cells & Alternative Fuels

Fuel cells and synthetic/alternative fuel generation.

Decarbonization

Defossilization of energy systems through hydrogen utilization.

Education & Training

Education and training programs on hydrogen technologies.

Conference History

ICH2P has a proud tradition of international scholarship since 2009.

2009 – 2025

Seoul, South Korea (2012)
Fukuoka, Japan (2014)

Hangzhou, China (2016)
Brisbane, Australia (2017)

About ICH2P 2026

De La Salle Lipa is proud to host the 17th International Conference on Hydrogen Production (ICH2P 2026), bringing together researchers, scientists, academicians, industry specialists, and professionals working in hydrogen energy technologies.

The conference has a key focus on hydrogen ecosystem development and decarbonization — critical priorities for countries worldwide — and serves as a forum for ideas, experience, and knowledge on hydrogen production, storage, delivery, distribution, conversion, and utilization.

ICH2P 2026 will be held at the Learning Resource Center, De La Salle Lipa, Lipa City, Batangas, Philippines, December 17–19, 2026.

Venue — De La Salle Lipa

Airport to DLSL Transportation Guide — from Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA), Manila.

Private Transfer / Taxi

After arriving at NAIA, take an airport taxi, metered taxi, or book a ride through transport applications such as Grab. The journey to De La Salle Lipa, Lipa City, Batangas takes approximately 1.5–3 hours, depending on traffic conditions.

By Bus (Recommended)

From NAIA, take a taxi or Grab to Buendia (Gil Puyat) Bus Terminal, PITX (Parañaque Integrated Terminal Exchange), or Cubao, where buses bound for Lipa City, Batangas are available. Board a bus going to SM Lipa, Lipa City, or Batangas City that passes through Lipa. Upon arrival at the Lipa City Bus Terminal or SM Lipa Transport Terminal, take a tricycle to De La Salle Lipa — approximately 10–15 minutes from either terminal.

By Rented Car

Participants who prefer to drive may rent a vehicle at NAIA through the airport's accredited car rental providers. The trip to De La Salle Lipa is via the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and STAR Tollway, with an estimated travel time of 1.5–2.5 hours, depending on traffic.

Conference Shuttle Service
The Organizing Committee will arrange a shuttle service for registered participants, subject to sufficient demand. Details regarding the schedule, designated pick-up point, and registration procedure will be announced closer to the conference dates.
